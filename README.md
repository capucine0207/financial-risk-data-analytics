# Financial Risk & Data Analytics Projects

This repository presents a selection of academic projects focused on financial risk, data analysis, and quantitative modeling within a banking environment.

The projects cover key areas such as model evaluation, portfolio optimization, and liquidity risk management, with applications aligned with real-world banking challenges.

---

## Projects

### 1. Airbus Return Prediction (R)
Development and comparison of statistical and machine learning models to predict stock returns.

- Models: OLS, Lasso, Random Forest, Boosting  
- Focus on model evaluation (RMSE, MAE, R²) and overfitting  
- Time series analysis in a structured R environment  

→ Folder: `airbus-return-prediction`

---

### 2. Portfolio Construction & Optimization (Excel)
Construction of optimal investment portfolios using quantitative finance methods.

- Efficient frontier and portfolio selection  
- Risk-return trade-off analysis  
- Sharpe ratio and utility-based optimization  
- Implementation in Excel  

→ Folder: `portfolio-management`

---

### 3. Liquidity Risk Management & Funding Optimization (Excel)
Analysis of funding needs and liquidity risk under banking constraints.

- Balance sheet analysis and cash flow projection  
- Liquidity coverage constraints by maturity  
- Optimization of funding strategy under cost constraints  
- Integration of uncertainty (defaults, irregular flows)  

→ Folder: `liquidity-risk-management`
