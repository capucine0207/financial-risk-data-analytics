# Liquidity Risk Management & Funding Optimization

## Objective
Determine optimal funding needs for a financial institution while minimizing refinancing costs and ensuring compliance with internal liquidity constraints.

## Context
This project is based on a banking case study involving balance sheet analysis, funding constraints, and liquidity risk management within a group structure.

## Approach
- Analysis of balance sheet structure and funding sources  
- Projection of future cash flows and funding needs  
- Application of internal liquidity coverage constraints by maturity  
- Consideration of different funding strategies (planned vs. non-planned funding)  

## Risk Analysis
- Integration of uncertainty related to loan defaults and irregular cash inflows  
- Stress considerations on repayment flows and funding needs  
- Evaluation of liquidity buffers and safety margins  

## Optimization
- Determination of optimal borrowing strategy (maturity and volume)  
- Minimization of refinancing cost under constraints  
- Trade-off between cost efficiency and liquidity risk coverage  

## Key Insights
- Importance of matching asset and liability maturities  
- Impact of uncertainty on liquidity planning  
- Cost implications of non-anticipated funding needs  

## Skills Demonstrated
- Liquidity risk management  
- Balance sheet analysis  
- Financial planning and forecasting  
- Risk analysis under uncertainty
