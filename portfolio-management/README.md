# Portfolio Construction & Optimization

## Objective
Construct and optimize a portfolio of equities using quantitative methods to analyze risk-return trade-offs and identify optimal investment strategies.

## Approach
- Equity screening based on financial, sectoral, and performance criteria  
- Selection of 10 assets based on performance and correlation analysis  
- Computation of weekly returns from historical price data  
- Construction of covariance and correlation matrices  
- Visualization of assets in the mean-variance space  

## Portfolio Construction
- Equal-weight (naïve) portfolio  
- Minimum Variance Portfolio (MVP)  
- Maximum Return Portfolio (MRP)  
- Efficient frontier construction  

## Optimization
- Identification of optimal risky portfolios based on risk aversion coefficients  
- Integration of a risk-free asset and construction of the Capital Allocation Line (CAL)  
- Analysis of complete portfolios under different risk preferences  
- Extension to short-selling constraints  

## Key Insights
- Importance of diversification and correlation in portfolio construction  
- Trade-off between risk and return across different portfolio strategies  
- Impact of risk aversion on optimal asset allocation  

## Skills Demonstrated
- Portfolio optimization  
- Risk-return analysis  
- Financial modeling in Excel  
- Quantitative analysis of financial data
